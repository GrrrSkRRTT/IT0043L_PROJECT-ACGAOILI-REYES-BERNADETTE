OUR STORY

At KapeTayo, coffee is more than just a drink—it's a way to connect. Inspired by the Filipino spirit of sama-samang kwentuhan over a warm cup, our cafe was built on the idea that the best conversations often begin with “Tara, kape tayo!”

What started as a small dream among friends has now become a cozy corner for students, workers, and coffee lovers in the community. From our locally sourced beans to our freshly baked pastries, we aim to bring you comfort in every sip and bite.
