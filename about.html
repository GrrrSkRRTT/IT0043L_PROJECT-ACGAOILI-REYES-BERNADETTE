ABOUT US

Welcome to KapeTayo — where every cup tells a story. Rooted in the Filipino culture of togetherness and kwentuhan over coffee, KapeTayo was founded with a simple goal: to create a space where people feel at home. Whether you're here for a quick caffeine fix, a quiet study nook, or a cozy catch-up with friends, you're always welcome at our table.

We take pride in every brew, using quality ingredients, locally inspired flavors, and a touch of pusong Pinoy in everything we do. From our warm service to our comforting menu, KapeTayo isn't just a place to drink coffee—it's a place to feel connected.

Mission

To serve great-tasting coffee and comfort food in a warm, welcoming environment that reflects the heart of Filipino hospitality. We aim to create moments of rest, connection, and joy—one cup at a time

Vision

To become a beloved neighborhood cafe brand that brings people together and celebrates Filipino culture through every brew, bite, and interaction—both offline and online.
